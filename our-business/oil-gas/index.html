Oil & Gas 

OceanWorks International is a leading supplier of systems, products and services to the Oil and Gas industry with customers located throughout the world.

OceanWorks provides customized solutions to create, develop, maintain and enhance specific oil and gas requirements and challenges. OceanWorks has built a reputation for systems level analysis and project execution to ensure our market offerings work right first time and keep on working to ensure minimal downtime and continuing to deliver on our reliability goals.

Our Oil & Gas offerings include; Power distribution, Control & Subsea modules. Enhanced Launch and Recovery solutions. Subsea Systems and continue Subsea support to ensure greater reliability and availability of installed equipment including continuous real-time remote monitoring of facilities and equipment.

Fabrication Services

 

OceanWorks International designs and builds subsea and marine equipment for clients all over the world. We specialize in the fabrication of subsea field development hardware, structures and tooling, including:

• ROV frames

• Tooling skids

• Spreader bars

• Pig launchers & receivers

• PLETs & PLEMs

• Choke & kill lines

• Jumpers & pipe spools

• Subsea skids

• Subsea skids, frames & structures

• Custom & standard ROV tooling

Welding Capabilities
     

OceanWorks offers a wide variety of welding capabilities to meet the stringent demands of the offshore and subsea industries. Among others, welding services meeting the following industry standards, processes and materials are offered:

• API 1104

• AWS D1.1/D1.2/D1.6

• ASME section IX

• GMAW (MIG)

• FCAW (Fluxcore)

• SMAW (Stick)

• GTAW (TIG)

• SAW (Subarc)

• Mild steels

• Stainless steels

• Duplex stainless steel

• Aluminum

 

For more information please contact: sales@oceanworks.com
